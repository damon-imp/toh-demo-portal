The Problem

Most health coaching runs on
eyeballed PDFs and guesswork.

You get a lab report once a year. Your provider reads it once. Nobody compares it to last year. Nobody flags the marker that's been trending wrong for 18 months. The stack you're on was built off a 15-minute call, not off your actual biology.

One Snapshot, No Trend

A single lab draw tells you where you are today. It can't tell you where you're headed. A testosterone of 450 ng/dL means one thing if you were at 620 last year. Something very different if you were at 380.

Missed Connections

Low energy, poor sleep, and brain fog don't live in one marker. They live across cortisol, thyroid, vitamin D, hormones, and inflammation. Nobody's looking at all of them at once.

Generic Protocols

The same peptide stack and supplement list handed to everyone in the program. Built off a template, not off your labs, your symptoms, or how you've responded to anything before.

No One Tracking the Response

You start a protocol. Then what? Most programs don't retest. They don't pivot. You either feel better or you don't, and nobody's looking at the numbers to know why.
